About Me

My name is Asiedu Messiah, born on 29th August 2003 in Atebubu, Bono-East Region, Ghana. I come from a loving family, with my father, Mr. Alexander Nkrumah, and mother, Yaa Badu. I have two wonderful sisters, Anita Andzie and Victoria Asiedu, and two handsome brothers, Amoako Stephen and Asiedu Justice Nkrumah.

Education

I attended New Era International Academy, New Apostolic Preparatory Junior High School, and New Academy School. For my senior high school education, I studied at Atebubu Senior High School, where the environment and supportive teachers helped me lay a good foundation, resulting in excellent outcomes in the West African Certificate Examination. I studied Business during this time.

Currently, I am pursuing a degree in Bachelor of Science in Information Technology at the prestigious University of Ghana (UG), Legon, where I'm deepening my knowledge in software development, networking, and data management.
